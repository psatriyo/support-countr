üì± About Countr

Countr is a strategic turn-based multiplayer game where players take turns incrementing a shared counter, with the goal of being the player who reaches a randomly-generated target number. Each player has a limited number of steps (taps) per turn, adding strategic depth to the gameplay.

Developer: Prayudi Satriyo
Version: 2.0
Requires: iOS 15.0 or later

üéÆ How to Play

Objective

Be the player who reaches the target count!

Game Rules

  1. 1
    Take Turns
    Players alternate trying to reach the target
  2. 2
    Tap 'Go!'
    Increment the counter by 1 (each tap uses one step)
  3. 3
    Watch Your Steps
    Each turn has 3-5 random steps you can use
  4. 4
    Switch Players
    Tap 'Switch!' to pass your turn (requires at least 1 step taken)
  5. 5
    Reach the Target
    The player who hits the randomly-generated target (10-25) wins!
üí° Strategy Tips
  • Count ahead to see if you can win on your current turn
  • Force your opponent into positions where they can't win
  • Save steps for crucial moments
  • Watch the math - if remaining count matches your steps, go for it!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How many players can play?
A: Countr supports 2-6 players in a single game.
Q: Can I customize players?
A: Yes! You can customize each player's name, emoji/character, and color.
Q: Is there a tutorial in the app?
A: Yes! Tap the "About" or "How to Play" button in the app for detailed instructions and rules.
Q: Is an internet connection required?
A: No. Countr is a local multiplayer game that works completely offline.

Technical Questions

Q: Why isn't sound playing?
A: Check that your device is not in silent mode. Countr includes both sound effects and haptic feedback. If sounds don't play, haptic feedback will still provide tactile responses.
Q: The app isn't responding. What should I do?
A: Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If problems persist, restart your device.
Q: What iOS version do I need?
A: Countr requires iOS 15.0 or later.
Q: Does Countr support Dark Mode?
A: Yes! Countr automatically adapts to your device's appearance settings.

Gameplay Questions

Q: Why can't I switch players?
A: You must take at least one step (tap "Go!" at least once) before you can switch to the next player.
Q: What happens if I use all my steps?
A: The turn automatically ends and switches to the next player.
Q: Can I restart a game?
A: Yes! Look for the "New Game" or restart option in the game interface.

üîß Troubleshooting
